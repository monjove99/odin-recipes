Overnight Oats with Strawberries and Greek Yogurt

Strawberry-yogurt overnight oats! An ultimate healthy pre-planned breakfast that is packed with fiber and protein. Garnish with granola, World of Chia Fruit Spread flavor, or fresh berries.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 2 cups almond milk
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup chopped fresh strawberries
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds

Directions

  1. Stir together oats, almond milk, and Greek yogurt in a bowl. Mix in strawberries, honey, and chia seeds until well combined. Spoon mixture into 3 glass jars with lids.
  2. Refrigerate the jars overnight to allow the oats to soften. Serve cold in the morning.
